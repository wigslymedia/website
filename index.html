Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? Review our pricing information, learn about our WiFi assessment services, see our proven expertise, or check our guarantee.

How long does the assessment take? +

Typical assessments take 1-2 days on-site depending on facility size. A 50,000 sq ft facility usually requires 2 days for comprehensive coverage and interference analysis.

What happens after the assessment? +

You'll receive a detailed report within 5 business days including heat maps, findings, prioritized recommendations, and budget estimates. We'll schedule a consultation call to review the results and answer any questions.

What's the typical cost for improvements? +

Improvement costs vary based on your specific needs. The assessment report includes budget estimates for different implementation phases, typically ranging from $5,000-$50,000 depending on scope. Many facilities see significant improvements with targeted AP placement adjustments costing under $10,000.

Do I need to prepare anything? +

We'll need facility access during business hours (or after-hours if preferred), basic network documentation if available, and a list of known problem areas. We'll coordinate all details before the assessment.

What if I choose not to move forward? +

There's no obligation. You keep the assessment report and all recommendations regardless of whether you proceed with implementation. The report is yours to use as needed.

Do you work with specific WiFi vendors? +

We're vendor-neutral. Our assessments work with any WiFi vendor (Cisco, Aruba, Ruckus, Ubiquiti, etc.). We provide recommendations based on your specific needs, not vendor preferences.
